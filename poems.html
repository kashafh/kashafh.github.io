Battles fought within are the loudest.

Your light is your superpower.

Everyone has a story with a different plot.

Beauty exists through all your seasons.

You will find the season of your soul.

Find peace in the absence of answers.

Someone being patient with you is a blessing.

Art of making peace through the chaos.

It takes time to become a great swimmer.

You just have to be patient with yourself.

Its hard to understand our own.

Growing up isnt as fun as we thought.
